Who I am?

Think of me as just an ordinary person, just like you.

In 2021, I decided to take a step into the world of programming, not as a career choice but simply as a hobby. I started small, experimenting with simple projects and gradually working my way up. One of my first milestones was creating a basic social network in PHP—a project that made me realize just how much I enjoy the process of building things from scratch.

Anyway I’m a 20-year-old who finds joy in crafting retro-style projects. For me, coding isn’t just about functionality; it’s about creating things that feel timeless and meaningful. I take great pride in working on things that bring me happiness, even if they’re not overly complex. It’s all about the journey and the satisfaction of seeing an idea come to life.

I have a deep love for animals, especially cats and foxes—they inspire me in their own way. I also enjoy finding beauty in simplicity, both in life and in the things I create. While I have a few minor speech issues, I’m constantly improving and not letting them hold me back.

This is who I am—just a simple guy with big dreams, a love for coding, and a soft spot for animals.

About [PowerPC] OpenMac

Ahh... this server was created to bring together a community passionate about building and creativity, with no imposed limits but guided by basic rules. The main idea is to provide a simple and friendly space where users can build and collaborate freely without the fear of griefing. We aim to inspire and support innovation in every project crafted here.

A brief idea of the server is as follows:

  • The server is inspired by Action Retro's idea, representing a promising start for a concept.

  • We preserve the worlds for as long as possible, keeping the server as active as we can. It serves as an archive for every build created by each member

  • The server might be offline for a few days or even more than a month, but it will come back online in one form or another (private or public).

  • I commit to maintaining the server as long as possible, but if you'd like to help, you can do so through Donate
